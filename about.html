Our Heritage

The Heart of the Spice Trade

Indonesia has been the epicenter of the global spice trade for centuries, with cloves native to the Maluku islands. Today, we continue this legacy by working directly with farming communities across the archipelago.

As a registered export company, CloveDirect handles the entire supply chain from farm gate to destination port. Our deep understanding of international trade regulations, customs procedures, and phytosanitary requirements guarantees smooth and reliable deliveries.

We believe in sustainable agriculture. By providing fair compensation and educational support to our farmers, we ensure the longevity of their plantations and the consistent quality of our supply.
